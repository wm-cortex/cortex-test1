Ecommerce - Payment Methods per Product

Overview

Are you want to link product with specific payment methods?
This module enables you easily to assign specific payment methods to product and once your customer purchase the product from your Ecommerce website, he can pay you with the methods selected in the product.

Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo Community Edition but not tested in Enterprise Edition.

Features
Ability to assign payment method(s) for each product.

How it works

You will add the payment acquires to the product from product form. Leaving the payment acquires field empty in product means show all enabled payment methods.

In Ecommerce, which payment methods will be shown?

  • If you added one product to cart and this product linked with payment acquires, then only selected payment acquires will be shown in payment page.
  • If you added two products to cart and these products linked with payment acquires, then all payment acquires selected in both products will be shown in payment page.
  • If you added any product which not linked to payment acquires, then all active payment acquires will be shown.
